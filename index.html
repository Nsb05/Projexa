Interpretation notes

This system is a deep learning research prototype intended to support education and exploratory analysis. It does not represent a segmented tumor boundary and must not be used as a definitive diagnostic marker or to replace expert radiologic interpretation.

This interface is for research and educational purposes only and has not undergone regulatory approval or formal clinical validation. Model outputs must not be used as the sole basis for diagnosis or treatment decisions.
